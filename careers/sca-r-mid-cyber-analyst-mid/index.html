SCA-R Mid/ Cyber Analyst Mid

Experience:

  • 5 years IT experience and 3 years in experience in Certification and Accreditation/A&A,
  • Knowledgeable and trained in RMF process, NIST SP 800-37, NIST SP 800-53, CNSSI 1253
  • Demonstrates mid-level experience in risk analysis, control validation, and as a Security Control Assessor Representative.
